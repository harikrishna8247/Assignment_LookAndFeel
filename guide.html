Common Houseplants Care Guide

Plant Name Watering Schedule Sunlight Requirement Common Issues
Fern Once a week Low light Over-watering
Spider Plant Every 10 days Indirect sunlight Root rot
Rubber Tree Twice a month Bright, indirect light Leaf loss
Aloe Vera Every 3 weeks Direct sunlight Leaf wilting
Peace Lily Once a week Low to moderate light Brown leaf tips
Pothos Weekly to bi-weekly Low to medium indirect light Yellowing leaves, overwatering
Devil's Ivy Weekly to bi-weekly Low to medium indirect light Browning leaves, overwatering
ZZ Plant Monthly Low to bright indirect light Yellowing leaves, overwatering
Philodendron Weekly to bi-weekly Low to medium indirect light Brown leaf tips, root rot
Chinese Money Plant Weekly, keep soil evenly moist but not soggy Indirect light Brown spots from direct sunlight, root rot from overwatering
Calathea Weekly, keep soil consistently moist Indirect light, avoid direct sunlight Brown leaf edges from dry air or direct sunlight, yellowing leaves from overwatering
Monstera Weekly to bi-weekly, allow soil to dry slightly between waterings Indirect light, can tolerate low light Brown leaf edges from dry air or direct sunlight, yellowing leaves from overwatering
Parlor Palm Every 1-2 weeks, allow soil to dry slightly between waterings Low to medium indirect light Browning leaves from overwatering, yellowing leaves from too much direct sunlight
